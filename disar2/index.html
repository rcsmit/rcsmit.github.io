Biography

DJ since 1995
From Sunset House to Peak Time Techno
United Baseline, Club Fuel, Club Sequence, Zenith and CrazyFM

Disar Marxin started his career in 1995. His love for electronic music and seamless blends quickly gained attention, building a strong name in the scene for his unique sound and selection.

After years in the commercial club circuit as both DJ and LJ, he followed his heart back to his passion for timeless, soulful grooves. If you caught him at Zenith or Beetlejuice around 2003, you felt the shift as commercial tunes made way for quality music.

Sharing the decks at legendary clubs like Fuel and Sequence with icons such as Laurent Warin, Michael Forzza, George's, and Phi-Phi shaped his ability to craft a true musical journey. Today, that deep understanding of crowd dynamics translates into his SUNSETS sound: uplifting, groovy, sun-drenched, and always open-minded.

It's all about the vibe, not the genre.

// Privacy Policy

1. Information I Collect

If you reach out, I collect your name, email address and/or phone-number, and your message. I adore self-deleting messages so please contact me on Signal!

I don't do website analytics so I don't use tools to understand how you use the site. If you do find something hidden on my site, PLEASE tell me so I can delete it because I don't like those things either.

Of course I have to collect your name, VAT-number, address and so on to make a contract and to invoice you.

2. How I Use Your Information

I use it to reply to your messages and I use it to make contracts and invoices, and to pay invoices. That's about it!

Invoices are made with Accountable. Check their privacy-policy. From legal obligation, invoices are primarily sent through Peppol.

My bank account is with Hello Bank.. Check their privacy-policy.

My mailserver is hosted at Hetzner Online GmbH in Germany. Check their privacy-policy.

All my documents (and your information) is stored only on premise on a server that is NOT accessible from the internet.

If I need to share documents with you, I also use a local server. It is, of course, accessible via the internet, but only through a personal link that expires once the file no longer needs to be shared.

I don't use so called big data service providers, not for file storage, not for office-suites, not for anything like that. Nothing but good news, right?

I never sell or share your personal information with third parties unless required by law.

3. Cookies

I do not use cookies, other than Lukken by Jules Destrooper. If I added them (by accident) somewhere, please let me know so I can destroy them!

4. Your Rights

You can:
- Ask to see what personal data I have about you
- Request corrections if the data is wrong
- Ask me to delete your data at any time (well... not the invoices of course, taxes wouldn't be happy if I do so)

Contact me at privacy@disar.be to exercise your rights.

5. Changes

I may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect immediately when posted.

6. Contact

For any questions about this policy, email me at privacy@disar.be.
