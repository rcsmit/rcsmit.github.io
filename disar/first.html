About Disar

Disar started his career in 1995. Because of his love for electronic music he became well-known very fast for his mixing style and choice of music.

After spending years in the commercial club scene, he followed his heart. People who knew him playing at ZENITH or Beetlejuice around 2003 noticed the shift toward deeper, quality music.

Working with DJs like Laurent Warin, Michael Forzza, Phi-Phi, and Cyril Schiancci shaped him into the DJ he is today: unique, deep, uplifting, and open-minded.
