Good questions

Is this for acroyoga festivals only?

No. Acroyoga is the clearest starting point, but the product is meant for retreats, trainings and community-led festivals with similar operational needs.

Why crowdfunding instead of fully bootstrapping?

Crowdfunding validates demand, creates a first circle of committed users and gives the project enough room to ship something solid instead of a rushed half-version.

What if the full target is not reached?

The build can still move forward in a tighter form. The scope stays focused on the core organizer dashboard, portal and event setup essentials.

Will backers shape the product?

Yes. Especially pilot organizers and launch partners. The best product decisions come from real event friction, not from abstract feature lists.

What if the project doesn't make it?

The honest answer is that you'd lose what you put in. Do not back with money that you can't afford or willing to loose.

Crowdfunding a software build is a real bet, not a pre-order. If FestFlow fails to reach its goals, runs out of runway, or has to shut down for whatever reason, your contribution cannot be recovered. There are no assets to liquidate, no refund mechanism, and no guarantee of any return — financial or otherwise.

Back this because you believe in what we're building and want to help make it exist — not because you expect a safety net. The best outcome for everyone is a product that actually gets built and used by thousands of enthousiast (acro)yogis and organizers. That's what your backing makes possible.
