FestFlow SaaS · Master Build Prompt v3

Multi-tenant platform
for acroyoga festivals

A full-stack SaaS for managing multi-day acroyoga and yoga festivals and retreats. One deployment serves many tenant organizations. Each tenant runs one or more events end-to-end — from teacher booking and schedule building to payments, logistics, and GDPR compliance.

Audiences & Apps
Role App Description
SiteOwner /apps/platform Platform operator. Manages tenants, billing, platform health. Never touches event content.
Organizer / Staff /apps/admin Tenant admin. Manages their event(s) end-to-end.
Teacher /apps/portal Sees their own schedule, manages their profile, views session participants.
Participant /apps/portal Registers, pays, manages schedule and profile.
Prospect /apps/website Public visitor. Discovers the event, registers.
