Why this matters

The problem is not passion. It is fragmented operations.

🧩

Too many tools

Organizers jump between forms, payment tools, email threads, spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, PDFs and public pages. Important details slip through.

⏱️

Too much manual work

Schedules, teacher info, ticketing, rooming, dietary notes, reminders and participant questions often get handled by hand. That eats the energy needed for the event itself.

🌿

Too little software that fits this world

Generic event tools miss retreat logic, festival flow, community culture, teacher needs and participant portals. Organizers need software that speaks their language.

FAQ

Good questions

Is this for acroyoga festivals only?

No. Acroyoga is the clearest starting point, but the product is meant for retreats, trainings and community-led festivals with similar operational needs.

Why crowdfunding instead of fully bootstrapping?

Crowdfunding validates demand, creates a first circle of committed users and gives the project enough room to ship something solid instead of a rushed half-version.

What if the full target is not reached?

The build can still move forward in a tighter form. The scope stays focused on the core organizer dashboard, portal and event setup essentials.

Will backers shape the product?

Yes. Especially pilot organizers and launch partners. The best product decisions come from real event friction, not from abstract feature lists.
