Retreat Overview
Everything you need to know about AcroFest 2025 — five days of movement, community, and connection in the heart of Bali.
🌿
You're joining 120 participants from 19 countries for five days of acroyoga classes, jams, workshops, performances, and shared meals at the Ubud Eco-Retreat. We're so happy you're here.

🤸 What to Expect
Each day has a morning flow, 2–3 workshops or classes across skill levels, an open jam session, and an evening gathering. The schedule is designed so you can go deep on specific skills or float freely between sessions. There's no pressure to attend everything — rest and play are equally valid choices.

The festival runs across five outdoor and indoor venues on the retreat grounds. All venues are within walking distance of each other.
🌐 Language
All sessions are in English. Facilitators speak a mix of English, Dutch, Portuguese, and Hebrew. Translation support available on request.
📶 Connectivity
WiFi available in communal areas and rooms. Signal is moderate — we encourage digital detox during sessions. WhatsApp group for daily updates.

Getting Here
Arrival & Departure
Everything you need for a smooth journey to and from Ubud.
✈️
The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International (DPS) in Denpasar, approximately 75 minutes from the retreat venue depending on traffic.
🚗 Getting to the Venue
Private taxi~350,000–450,000 IDR from airport. Pre-book via your hotel or use Grab app.
Shared transferWe've arranged group transfers on Wed 14 May at 12:00 and 15:00 from DPS. Reply to your confirmation email to reserve a seat (free of charge).
Scooter rentalAvailable in Ubud town center. Not recommended from airport with luggage.
From Ubud center~15 min by Grab or taxi. Tell the driver "Ubud Eco-Retreat, Jl. Raya Ubud."

🌍 Visa & Entry
Most nationalities receive a free 30-day visa on arrival at Bali airport. Check your country's requirements at imigrasi.go.id. Bring a printed or digital copy of your return flight and proof of accommodation.
Where You're Staying
Accommodation
The retreat offers several room types across the eco-retreat grounds, all within walking distance of all venues.
🏡 Private Bungalow
Premium
En-suite bathroom, AC, fan, private terrace overlooking rice fields. King or twin bed. Towels, toiletries, and daily housekeeping included.
🛏️ Shared Room (4-bed)
Standard
Shared bathroom (2 per room), fan, locker with key, community atmosphere. Great for solo travelers. Towels provided.

WiFiAvailable in all rooms and communal areas
PowerIndonesian plugs (Type C/F). Bring a universal adapter.
WaterFiltered drinking water stations — please bring a reusable bottle
LaundryLaundry service available at reception (paid separately)
💧
The retreat is an eco-property. Please use water and electricity mindfully. AC is available but we encourage fans where comfortable.

Extras
Optional Add-Ons
Enhance your festival experience with these optional extras. Book at reception on arrival.
🧘
Private Yoga Session 150k IDR / 60 min
One-on-one session with Elena M. or Ama F. Tailored to your needs — therapeutic, alignment-focused, or pranayama. Available mornings before 08:00 and evenings after 20:00.
🏍️
Scooter Rental 80k IDR / day
Explore Ubud and surroundings on a semi-automatic scooter. Helmet included. Valid license required. Available Wed–Sat, return by 18:00.
🌾
Rice Field Walk & Warung Lunch 120k IDR / person
Guided 2-hour walk through the Tegallalang rice terraces, ending at a local warung for a traditional Balinese lunch. Departing Thu & Sat at 08:30.
💆
Balinese Massage 180k IDR / 90 min
Traditional Balinese massage by local therapists. Available daily 15:00–20:00. Book at reception — slots fill fast on Fri & Sat.
🎨
Batik Workshop 200k IDR / person
2-hour traditional Balinese batik cloth painting workshop led by a local artist. Take your piece home. Thu afternoon 14:00–16:00. Max 12 participants.
ℹ️
All prices in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). ~15,000 IDR = €1. Cash preferred for most add-ons. ATM available 5 min walk from venue.

Movement & More
Activities & Experiences
What's on offer across the five days — from structured workshops to open exploration.
🤸
Acroyoga Classes Included
Daily structured classes across beginner, intermediate, and advanced tracks. Covering base technique, flying, washing machines, and therapeutic sequences.
🎵
Open Jam Sessions Included
Unstructured practice time with music. Spotters available. All levels welcome. The heart of every festival day — come with curiosity, not a plan.
🙌
Handstand Lab Included
Two sessions on Thu and Fri for beginners. Wrist prep, wall work, bail technique, and line coaching. No prior experience needed.
🎭
Performance Showcase Sat evening
90-minute showcase of participant-created pieces and invited artists. If you'd like to perform, sign up at the board near reception by Friday noon.
💬
Community Talks Included
Thu evening: Consent & Communication with Sarah K. Informal, open circle format. Attendance optional but warmly encouraged for all.
