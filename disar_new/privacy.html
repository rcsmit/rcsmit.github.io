Privacy policy

1. Information I Collect

If you reach out, I collect your name, email address and/or phone-number, and your message. I adore self-deleting messages so please contact me on Signal!

I don't do website analytics so I don't use tools like Google Analytics to understand how you use the site. If you do find something hidden on my site, PLEASE tell me so I can delete it because I don't like those things either.

Of course I have to collect your name, VAT-number, address and so on to make a contract and to invoice you.

2. How I Use Your Information

I use it to reply to your messages and i use it to make contracts and invoices, and to pay invoices. That's about it!

Invoices are made with Accountable. Check their privacy-policy. From legal obligation, invoices are primarily sent through Peppol. My mailserver is hosted at Hetzner Online GmbH in Germany. Check their privacy-policy.
All my documents (and your information) is stored on premise on a server that is NOT accessible from the internet.
If I have to share documents with you, I also use an on premise server. Of course that one is accessibel from the internet.

I don't use so called big data service providers, not for file storage, not for office-suites, not for anything like that. Nothing but good news, right?

I never sell or share your personal information with third parties unless required by law.

3. Cookies

I do not use cookies, other than Lukken by Jules Destrooper. If I added them (by accident) somewhere, please let me know so I can destroy them!

I have embedded some widgets like youtube-movies and those bastards are adding trackers and cookies... Please BLOCK THEM! I use Brave as a browser and added uBlock Origin and all cookies are gone now! My website doesn't need them and you can watch the embedded movies without them!

4. Your Rights

You can:
- Ask to see what personal data we have about you
- Request corrections if the data is wrong
- Ask us to delete your data at any time (well... not the invoices of course, taxes wouldn't be happy if I do so)

Contact me at privacy@disar.be to exercise your rights.

5. Changes

I may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect immediately when posted.

6. Contact

For any questions about this policy, email me at privacy@disar.be.
