The Four Angles of the Figure
ASC — Ascendant
Rising Sign · Eastern Horizon
Self, body & identity. The mask worn at first meeting. How others perceive you before they know you.
MC — Midheaven
Medium Coeli · Highest Point
Career, reputation & public role. Ambitions and the image projected into the world.
DSC — Descendant
Setting Sign · Western Horizon
Partnerships, marriage & others. What we seek in — and project onto — close companions.
IC — Imum Coeli
Nadir · Lowest Point
Home, roots & private life. Family origins, inner foundations, and the place of retreat.
