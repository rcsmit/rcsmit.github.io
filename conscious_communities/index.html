What is this?

An interactive world map of conscious communities, ecovillages, and alternative living spaces — gathered from forum discussions, Facebook groups, and personal research.

Use the layer panel on the left to filter by type, the continent tags to zoom into a region, or the search bar to find a specific place.

How to use

  • Click any marker to see details about the location
  • Toggle layers on/off using the sidebar checkboxes
  • Use ⭐ Shortlist to see personally recommended spots
  • Zoom in past level 6 to see individual name labels
  • Click the ◀ button on the map edge to collapse the sidebar
