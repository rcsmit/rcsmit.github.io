About My Journey

My professional journey began in a renowned restaurant within a major theme park in France. Not only did I find contentment in my work, but I also delighted guests with my service.

However, a persistent thought lingered: there must be a deeper fulfillment. I believed I could embrace greater joy and play a more impactful role in the world!

Consequently, I made the bold decision to depart from my stable job and chart a new path.

Interestingly, I embarked on this journey not just once, but twice! Each time, I discovered that despite returning to a familiar point, we continue evolving.

Through the highs and lows, I now live a life that was once merely a dream, currently seated on a tropical island beach, embracing the life I envisioned.

You, too, can carve this path!
